## Key takeaways (for thesis)

- **Why scipy SLSQP?** Portfolio variance minimization is a convex quadratic problem with linear constraints → guaranteed global minimum, SLSQP is the standard solver
- **Fat tails in VN30:** Positive kurtosis across all 30 tickers violates MPT's normality assumption → Chapter 5 limitation; CVaR (from Palomar) is the theoretical fix but out of scope for P1
